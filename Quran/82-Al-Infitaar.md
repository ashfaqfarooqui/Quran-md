# 1

Izas samaaa'un fatarat ^qTrans810


WHEN THE SKY is cleft asunder, ^qAsad810


When the heaven is cleft asunder, ^qPickthall810


When heaven is split open, ^qArberry810

# 2

Wa izal kawaakibun tasarat ^qTrans811


and when the stars are scattered, ^qAsad811


When the planets are dispersed, ^qPickthall811


when the stars are scattered, ^qArberry811

# 3

Wa izal bihaaru fujjirat ^qTrans812


and when the seas burst beyond their bounds, ^qAsad812


When the seas are poured forth, ^qPickthall812


when the seas swarm over, ^qArberry812

# 4

Wa izal qubooru bu'sirat ^qTrans813


and when the graves are overturned – ^qAsad813


And the sepulchres are overturned, ^qPickthall813


when the tombs are overthrown, ^qArberry813

# 5

'Alimat nafsum maa qaddamat wa akhkharat ^qTrans814


every human being will [at last] comprehend, what he has sent ahead and what he has held back [in this world]. ^qAsad814


A soul will know what it hath sent before (it) and what left behind. ^qPickthall814


then a soul shall know its works, the former and the latter. ^qArberry814

# 6

Yaaa ayyuhal insaaanu maa gharraka bi Rabbikal kareem ^qTrans815


O MAN! What is it that lures thee away from thy bountiful Sustainer, ^qAsad815


O man! What hath made thee careless concerning thy Lord, the Bountiful, ^qPickthall815


O Man! What deceived thee as to thy generous Lord ^qArberry815

# 7

Allazee khalaqaka fasaw waaka fa'adalak ^qTrans816


who has created thee, and formed thee in accordance with what thou art meant' to be, and shaped thy nature in just proportions, ^qAsad816


Who created thee, then fashioned, then proportioned thee? ^qPickthall816


who created thee and shaped thee and wrought thee in symmetry ^qArberry816

# 8

Feee ayye sooratim maa shaaa'a rakkabak ^qTrans817


having put thee together in whatever form He willed [thee to have]? ^qAsad817


Into whatsoever form He will, He casteth thee. ^qPickthall817


and composed thee after what form He would? ^qArberry817

# 9

Kalla bal tukazziboona bid deen ^qTrans818


Nay, [O men,] but you [are lured away from God whenever you are tempted to] give the lie to [God’s] Judgment! ^qAsad818


Nay, but ye deny the Judgment. ^qPickthall818


No indeed; but you cry lies to the Doom; ^qArberry818

# 10

Wa inna 'alaikum lahaa fizeen ^qTrans819


And yet, verily, there are ever-watchful forces over you, ^qAsad819


Lo! there are above you guardians, ^qPickthall819


yet there are over you watchers ^qArberry819

# 11

Kiraaman kaatibeen ^qTrans8110


noble, recording, ^qAsad8110


Generous and recording, ^qPickthall8110


noble, writers ^qArberry8110

# 12

Ya'lamoona ma taf'aloon ^qTrans8111


aware of whatever you do! ^qAsad8111


Who know (all) that ye do. ^qPickthall8111


who know whatever you do. ^qArberry8111

# 13

Innal abraara lafee na'eem ^qTrans8112


Behold, [in the life to come] the truly virtuous: will indeed be in bliss, ^qAsad8112


Lo! the righteous verily will be in delight. ^qPickthall8112


Surely the pious shall be in bliss, ^qArberry8112

# 14

Wa innal fujjaara lafee jaheem ^qTrans8113


whereas, behold, the wicked will indeed be in a blazing fire – ^qAsad8113


And lo! the wicked verily will be in hell; ^qPickthall8113


and the libertines shall be in a fiery furnace ^qArberry8113

# 15

Yaslawnahaa Yawmad Deen ^qTrans8114


[a fire] which they shall enter on Judgment Day, ^qAsad8114


They will burn therein on the Day of Judgment, ^qPickthall8114


roasting therein on the Day of Doom, ^qArberry8114

# 16

Wa maa hum 'anhaa bighaaa 'ibeen ^qTrans8115


and which they shall not [be able to] evade. ^qAsad8115


And will not be absent thence. ^qPickthall8115


nor shall they ever be absent from it. ^qArberry8115

# 17

Wa maaa adraaka maa Yawmud Deen ^qTrans8116


And what could make thee conceive what that Judgment Day will be? ^qAsad8116


Ah, what will convey unto thee what the Day of Judgment is! ^qPickthall8116


And what shall teach thee what is the Day of Doom? ^qArberry8116

# 18

Summa maaa adraaka maa Yawmud Deen ^qTrans8117


And once again: What could make thee conceive what that Judgment Day will be? ^qAsad8117


Again, what will convey unto thee what the Day of Judgment is! ^qPickthall8117


Again, what shall teach thee what is the Day of Doom? ^qArberry8117

# 19

Yawma laa tamliku nafsul linafsin shai'anw walamru yawma'izil lillaah ^qTrans8118


[It will be] a Day when no human being shall be of the least avail to another human being: for on that Day [it will become manifest that] all sovereignty is God's alone. ^qAsad8118


A day on which no soul hath power at all for any (other) soul. The (absolute) command on that day is Allah's. ^qPickthall8118


A day when no soul shall possess aught to succour another soul; that day the Command shall belong unto God. ^qArberry8118
