# 1

Hal ataa 'alal insaani heenum minad dahri lam yakun shai'am mazkooraa ^qTrans750


HAS THERE [not] been an endless span of time before man [appeared - a time] when he was not yet a thing to be thought of? ^qAsad750


Hath there come upon man (ever) any period of time in which he was a thing unremembered? ^qPickthall750


Has there come on man a while of time when he was a thing unremembered? ^qArberry750

# 2

Innaa khalaqnal insaana min nutfatin amshaajin nabta leehi faja'alnaahu samee'am baseeraa ^qTrans751


Verily, it is We who have created man out of a drop of sperm intermingled, so that We might try him [in his later life]: and therefore We made him a being endowed with hearing and sight. ^qAsad751


Lo! We create man from a drop of thickened fluid to test him; so We make him hearing, knowing. ^qPickthall751


We created man of a sperm-drop, a mingling, and We made him hearing, seeing. ^qArberry751

# 3

Innaa hadainaahus sabeela immaa shaakiranw wa immaa kafoora ^qTrans752


Verily, We have shown him the way: [and it rests with him to prove himself] either grateful or ungrateful. ^qAsad752


Lo! We have shown him the way, whether he be grateful or disbelieving. ^qPickthall752


Surely We guided him upon the way whether he be thankful or unthankful. ^qArberry752

# 4

Innaaa a'tadnaa lilkaa fireena salaasila wa aghlaalanw wa sa'eeraa ^qTrans753


[Now,] behold, for those who deny the truth We have readied chains and shackles, and a blazing flame ^qAsad753


Lo! We have prepared for disbelievers manacles and carcans and a raging fire. ^qPickthall753


Surely We have prepared for the unbelievers chains, fetters, and a Blaze. ^qArberry753

# 5

innal abraara yashra boona min kaasin kaana mizaa juhaa kaafooraa ^qTrans754


[whereas,] behold, the truly virtuous shall drink from a cup flavoured with the calyx of sweet-smelling flowers: ^qAsad754


Lo! the righteous shall drink of a cup whereof the mixture is of Kafur, ^qPickthall754


Surely the pious shall drink of a cup whose mixture is camphor, ^qArberry754

# 6

'Aynany yashrabu bihaa 'ibaadul laahi yafajjiroonahaa tafjeeraa ^qTrans755


a source [of bliss] whereof God's servants shall drink, seeing it flow in a flow abundant. ^qAsad755


A spring wherefrom the slaves of Allah drink, making it gush forth abundantly, ^qPickthall755


a fountain whereat drink the servants of God, making it to gush forth plenteously. ^qArberry755

# 7

Yoofoona binnazri wa yakhaafoona yawman kaana sharruhoo mustateeraa ^qTrans756


[The truly virtuous are] they [who] fulfil their vows, and stand in awe of a Day the woe of which is bound to spread far and wide, ^qAsad756


(Because) they perform the vow and fear a day whereof the evil is wide-spreading, ^qPickthall756


They fulfil their vows, and fear a day whose evil is upon the wing; ^qArberry756

# 8

Wa yut''imoonat ta'aama 'alaa hubbihee miskeenanw wa yatemanw wa aseeraa ^qTrans757


and who give food - however great be their own want of it - unto the needy, and the orphan, and the captive, ^qAsad757


And feed with food the needy wretch, the orphan and the prisoner, for love of Him, ^qPickthall757


they give food, for the love of Him, to the needy, the orphan, the captive: ^qArberry757

# 9

Innaamaa nut'imukum li wajhil laahi laa nureedu minkum jazaaa'anw wa laa shukooraa ^qTrans758


[saying, in their hearts,] "We feed you for the sake of God alone: we desire no recompense from you, nor thanks: ^qAsad758


(Saying): We feed you, for the sake of Allah only. We wish for no reward nor thanks from you; ^qPickthall758


'We feed you only for the Face of God; we desire no recompense from you, no thankfulness; ^qArberry758

# 10

Innaa nakhaafu mir Rabbinna Yawman 'aboosan qamtareeraa ^qTrans759


behold, we stand in awe of our Sustainer's judgment on a distressful, fateful Day!" ^qAsad759


Lo! we fear from our Lord a day of frowning and of fate. ^qPickthall759


for we fear from our Lord a frowning day, inauspicious. ^qArberry759

# 11

Fa waqaahumul laahu sharra zaalikal yawmi wa laqqaahum nadratanw wa surooraa ^qTrans7510


And so, God will preserve them from the woes of that Day, and will bestow on them brightness and joy, ^qAsad7510


Therefor Allah hath warded off from them the evil of that day, and hath made them find brightness and joy; ^qPickthall7510


So God has guarded them from the evil of that day, and has procured them radiancy and gladness, ^qArberry7510

# 12

Wa jazaahum bimaa sabaroo janatanw wa hareeraa ^qTrans7511


and will reward them for all their patience in adversity with a garden [of bliss] and with [garments of] silk. ^qAsad7511


And hath awarded them for all that they endured, a Garden and silk attire; ^qPickthall7511


and recompensed them for their patience with a Garden, and silk; ^qArberry7511

# 13

muttaki'eena feeha 'alal araaa 'iki laa yarawna feehaa shamsanw wa laa zamhareeraa ^qTrans7512


In that [garden] they will on couches recline, and will know therein neither [burning] sun nor cold severe, ^qAsad7512


Reclining therein upon couches, they will find there neither (heat of) a sun nor bitter cold. ^qPickthall7512


therein they shall recline upon couches, therein they shall see neither sun nor bitter cold; ^qArberry7512

# 14

Wa daaniyatan 'alaihim zilaaluhaa wa zullilat qutoofu haa tazleela ^qTrans7513


since its [blissful] shades will come down low over them, and low will hang down its clusters of fruit, most easy to reach. ^qAsad7513


The shade thereof is close upon them and the clustered fruits thereof bow down. ^qPickthall7513


near them shall be its shades, and its clusters hung meekly down, ^qArberry7513

# 15

Wa yutaafu 'alaihim bi aaniyatim min fiddatinw wa akwaabin kaanat qawaareeraa ^qTrans7514


And they will be waited upon with vessels of silver and goblets that will [seem to] be crystal – ^qAsad7514


Goblets of silver are brought round for them, and beakers (as) of glass ^qPickthall7514


and there shall be passed around them vessels of silver, and goblets of crystal, ^qArberry7514

# 16

Qawaareera min fiddatin qaddaroohaa taqdeeraa ^qTrans7515


crystal-like, [but] of silver - the measure whereof they alone will determine. ^qAsad7515


(Bright as) glass but (made) of silver, which they (themselves) have measured to the measure (of their deeds). ^qPickthall7515


crystal of silver that they have measured -- very exactly. ^qArberry7515

# 17

Wa yusawna feehaa kaasan kaana mizaajuhaa zanjabeelaa ^qTrans7516


And in that [paradise] they will be given to drink of a cup flavoured with ginger, ^qAsad7516


There are they watered with a cup whereof the mixture is of Zanjabil, ^qPickthall7516


And therein they shall be given to drink a cup whose mixture is ginger, ^qArberry7516

# 18

'Aynan feeha tusammaa salsabeelaa ^qTrans7517


[derived from] a source [to be found] therein, whose name is "Seek Thy Way". ^qAsad7517


(The water of) a spring therein, named Salsabil. ^qPickthall7517


therein a fountain whose name is called Salsabil. ^qArberry7517

# 19

Wa yatoofu 'alaihim wildaanum mukhalladoona izaa ra aytahum hasibtahum lu'lu 'am mansoora ^qTrans7518


And immortal youths will wait upon them: when thou seest them, thou wouldst deem them to be scattered pearls; ^qAsad7518


There wait on them immortal youths, whom, when thou seest, thou wouldst take for scattered pearls. ^qPickthall7518


Immortal youths shall go about them; when thou seest them, thou supposest them scattered pearls, ^qArberry7518

# 20

Wa izaa ra ayta summa ra ayta na'eemanw wa mulkan kabeera ^qTrans7519


and when thou seest [anything that is] there thou wilt see [only] bliss and a realm transcendent ^qAsad7519


When thou seest, thou wilt see there bliss and high estate. ^qPickthall7519


when thou seest them then thou seest bliss and a great kingdom. ^qArberry7519

# 21

'Aaliyahum siyaabu sundusin khudrunw wa istabraq, wa hullooo asaawira min fiddatinw wa saqaahum Rabbuhum sharaaban tahooraa ^qTrans7520


Upon those [blest] will be garments of green silk and brocade; and they will be adorned with bracelets of silver. And their Sustainer will them to drink of a drink most pure. ^qAsad7520


Their raiment will be fine green silk and gold embroidery. Bracelets of silver will they wear. Their Lord will slake their thirst with a pure drink. ^qPickthall7520


Upon them shall be green garments of silk and brocade; they are adorned with bracelets of silver, and their Lord shall give them to drink a pure draught. ^qArberry7520

# 22

Innaa haazaa kaana lakum jazz 'anw wa kaana sa'yukum mashkooraa ^qTrans7521


[And they will be told:] "Verily, all this is your reward since Your endeavour [in life] has met [God's] goodly acceptance!" ^qAsad7521


(And it will be said unto them): Lo! this is a reward for you. Your endeavour (upon earth) hath found acceptance. ^qPickthall7521


Behold, this is a recompense for you, and your striving is thanked.' ^qArberry7521

# 23

Innaa nahnu nazzalnaa 'alaikal quraana tanzeelaa ^qTrans7522


VERILY, [O believer,] it is We who have bestowed from on high this Qur’an upon thee, step by step - truly a bestowal from on high! ^qAsad7522


Lo! We, even We, have revealed unto thee the Qur'an, a revelation; ^qPickthall7522


Surely We have sent down the Koran on thee, a sending down; ^qArberry7522

# 24

Fasbir lihukmi Rabbika wa laa tuti' minhum aasiman aw kafooraa ^qTrans7523


Await, then, in all patience thy Sustainer's judgment, and pay no heed to any of them, who is a wilful sinner or an ingrate; ^qAsad7523


So submit patiently to thy Lord's command, and obey not of them any guilty one or disbeliever. ^qPickthall7523


so be thou patient under the judgment of thy Lord, and obey not one of them, sinner or unbeliever. ^qArberry7523

# 25

Wazkuris ma Rabbika bukratanw wa aseelaa ^qTrans7524


and bear in mind thy Sustainer's name at morn and evening ^qAsad7524


Remember the name of thy Lord at morn and evening. ^qPickthall7524


And remember the Name of thy Lord at dawn and in the evening ^qArberry7524

# 26

Wa minal laili fasjud lahoo wa sabbihhu lailan taweelaa ^qTrans7525


and during some of the night, and prostrate thyself before Him, and extol His limitless glory throughout the long night. ^qAsad7525


And worship Him (a portion) of the night. And glorify Him through the livelong night. ^qPickthall7525


and part of the night; bow down before Him and magnify Him through the long night. ^qArberry7525

# 27

Inna haaa'ulaa'i yuhibboona 'aajilata wa yazaroona waraaa'ahum yawman saqeelaa ^qTrans7526


Behold, they [who are unmindful of God] love this fleeting life, and leave behind them [all thought of] a grief-laden Day. ^qAsad7526


Lo! these love fleeting life, and put behind them (the remembrance of) a grievous day. ^qPickthall7526


Surely these men love the hasty world, and leave be behind them a heavy day. ^qArberry7526

# 28

Nahnu khalaqnaahum wa shadadnaaa asrahum wa izaa shi'naa baddalnaaa amsaala hum tabdeelaa ^qTrans7527


[They will not admit to themselves that] it We who have created them and strengthened their make - and [that] if it be Our will We can replace them entirely with others of their kind. ^qAsad7527


We, even We, created them, and strengthened their frame. And when We will, We can replace them, bringing others like them in their stead. ^qPickthall7527


We created them, and We strengthened their joints; and, when We will, We shall exchange their likes. ^qArberry7527

# 29

Inna haazihee tazkiratun fa man shaaa'at takhaza ilaa rabbihee sabeela ^qTrans7528


VERILY, all this is an admonition: whoever, then, so wills, may unto his Sustainer find a way. ^qAsad7528


Lo! this is an Admonishment, that whosoever will may choose a way unto his Lord. ^qPickthall7528


Surely this is a Reminder; so he who will, takes unto his Lord a way. ^qArberry7528

# 30

Wa maa tashaaa'oona illaa anyyashaaa'al laah; innal laahaa kaana'Aleeman Hakeema ^qTrans7529


But you cannot will it unless God wills [to show you that way]: for, behold, God is indeed all-seeing, wise. ^qAsad7529


Yet ye will not, unless Allah willeth. Lo! Allah is Knower, Wise. ^qPickthall7529


But you will not unless God wills; surely God is ever All-knowing, All-wise. ^qArberry7529

# 31

Yudkhilu mai yashaaa'u fee rahmatih; wazzaalimeena a'adda lahum 'azaaban aleemaa ^qTrans7530


He admits unto His grace everyone who wills [to be admitted]; but as for the evildoers - for them has He readied grievous suffering [in the life to come]. ^qAsad7530


He maketh whom He will to enter His mercy, and for evil-doers hath prepared a painful doom. ^qPickthall7530


For He admits into His mercy whomsoever He will; as for the evildoers, He has prepared for them a painful chastisement. ^qArberry7530
