# 1

Tabaarakal lazee biyadihil mulku wa huwa 'alaa kulli shai-in qadeer ^qTrans660


HALLOWED be He in whose hand all dominion rests, since He has the power to will anything: ^qAsad660


Blessed is He in Whose hand is the Sovereignty, and, He is Able to do all things. ^qPickthall660


Blessed be He in whose hand is the Kingdom -- He is powerful over everything -- ^qArberry660

# 2

Allazee khalaqal mawta walhayaata liyabluwakum ayyukum ahsanu 'amalaa; wa huwal 'azeezul ghafoor ^qTrans661


He who has created death as well as life, so that He might put you to a test [and thus show] which of you is best in conduct, and [make you realize that] He alone is almighty, truly forgiving. ^qAsad661


Who hath created life and death that He may try you which of you is best in conduct; and He is the Mighty, the Forgiving, ^qPickthall661


who created death and life, that He might try you which of you is fairest in works; and He is the All-mighty, the All-forgiving -- ^qArberry661

# 3

Allazee khalaqa sab'a samaawaatin tibaaqam maa taraa fee khalqir rahmaani min tafaawutin farji'il basara hal taraa min futoor ^qTrans662


[Hallowed be] He who has created seven heavens in full harmony with one another: no fault will thou see in the creation of the Most Gracious. And turn thy vision [upon it] once more: canst thou see any flaw? ^qAsad662


Who hath created seven heavens in harmony. Thou (Muhammad) canst see no fault in the Beneficent One's creation; then look again: Canst thou see any rifts? ^qPickthall662


who created seven heavens one upon another. Thou seest not in the creation of the All-merciful any imperfection. Return thy gaze; seest thou any fissure? ^qArberry662

# 4

Summar ji'il basara karrataini yanqalib ilaikal basaru khaasi'anw wa huwa haseer ^qTrans663


Yea, turn thy vision [upon it] again and yet again: [and every time] thy vision will fall back upon thee, dazzled and truly defeated…. ^qAsad663


Then look again and yet again, thy sight will return unto thee weakened and made dim. ^qPickthall663


Then return thy gaze again, and again, and thy gaze comes back to thee dazzled, aweary. ^qArberry663

# 5

Wa laqad zaiyannas samaaa'ad dunyaa bimasaa beeha wa ja'alnaahaa rujoomal lish shayaateeni wa a'tadnaa lahum 'azaabas sa'eer ^qTrans664


And, indeed, We have adorned the skies nearest to the earth with lights, and have made them the object of futile guesses for the evil ones [from among men]: and for them have We readied suffering through a blazing flame – ^qAsad664


And verily We have beautified the world's heaven with lamps, and We have made them missiles for the devils, and for them We have prepared the doom of flame. ^qPickthall664


And We adorned the lower heaven with lamps, and made them things to stone Satans; and We have prepared for them the chastisement of the Blaze. ^qArberry664

# 6

Wa lillazeena kafaroo bi rabbihim 'azaabu jahannama wa bi'sal maseer ^qTrans665


for, suffering in hell awaits all who are [thus] bent on blaspheming against their Sustainer: and how vile a journey's end! ^qAsad665


And for those who disbelieve in their Lord there is the doom of hell, a hapless journey's end! ^qPickthall665


And for those who disbelieve in their Lord there awaits the chastisement of Gehenna -- an evil homecoming! ^qArberry665

# 7

Izaaa ulqoo feehaa sami'oo lahaa shaheeqanw wa hiya tafoor ^qTrans666


When they are cast into that [hell], they will hear its breath indrawing as it boils up, ^qAsad666


When they are flung therein they hear its roaring as it boileth up, ^qPickthall666


When they are cast into it they will hear it sighing, the while it boils ^qArberry666

# 8

Takaadu tamayyazu minal ghaizi kullamaaa uliqya feehaa fawjun sa alahum khazanatuhaaa alam yaatikum nazeer ^qTrans667


well-nigh bursting with fury; [and] every time a host [of such sinners] is flung into it, its keepers will ask them, "Has no warner ever come to you?" ^qAsad667


As it would burst with rage. Whenever a (fresh) host is flung therein the wardens thereof ask them: Came there unto you no warner? ^qPickthall667


and wellnigh bursts asunder with rage. As often as a troop is cast into it, its keepers ask them, 'Came there no warner to you?'; ^qArberry667

# 9

Qaaloo balaa qad jaaa'anaa nazeerun fakazzabnaa wa qulnaa maa nazzalal laahu min shai in in antum illaa fee dalaalin kabeer ^qTrans668


They will reply: "Yea, a warner did indeed come unto us, but we gave him the lie and said, 'Never has God sent down anything [by way of revelation]! You [self-styled warners] are but lost in a great delusion!"' ^qAsad668


They say: Yea, verily, a warner came unto us; but we denied and said: Allah hath naught revealed; ye are in naught but a great error. ^qPickthall668


They say, 'Yes indeed, a warner came to us; but we cried lies, saying, "God has not sent down anything; you are only in great error. ^qArberry668

# 10

Wa qaaloo law kunnaa nasma'u awna'qilu maa kunnaa feee as haabis sa'eer ^qTrans669


And they will add: "Had we but listened [to those warnings], or [at least] used our own reason, we would not [now] be among those who are destined for the blazing flame!" ^qAsad669


And they say: Had we been wont to listen or have sense, we had not been among the dwellers in the flames. ^qPickthall669


They also say, 'If we had only heard, or had understood, we would not have been of the inhabitants of the Blaze.' ^qArberry669

# 11

Fa'tarafoo bizambihim fasuhqal li as haabis sa'eer ^qTrans6610


Thus will they come to realize their sins: but [by that time,] remote will have become all good from those who are destined for the blazing flame. ^qAsad6610


So they acknowledge their sins; but far removed (from mercy) are the dwellers in the flames. ^qPickthall6610


So they confess their sins. Curse the inhabitants of the Blaze! ^qArberry6610

# 12

Innal lazeena yakhshawna rabbahum bilghaibi lahum maghfiratunw wa ajrun kabeer ^qTrans6611


[As against this,] behold, for those who stand in awe of God although He is beyond the reach of their perception, there is forgiveness in store and a great reward. ^qAsad6611


Lo! those who fear their Lord in secret, theirs will be forgiveness and a great reward. ^qPickthall6611


Surely those who fear their Lord in the Unseen -- there awaits them forgiveness and a great wage. ^qArberry6611

# 13

Wa asirroo qawlakum awijharoo bihee innahoo 'aleemum bizaatis sudoor ^qTrans6612


AND [know, O men, that] whether you keep your beliefs secret or state them openly, He has full knowledge indeed of all that is in [your] hearts. ^qAsad6612


And keep your opinion secret or proclaim it, lo! He is Knower of all that is in the breasts (of men). ^qPickthall6612


Be secret in your speech, or proclaim it, He knows the thoughts within the breasts. ^qArberry6612

# 14

Alaa ya'lamu man khalaq wa huwal lateeful khabeer ^qTrans6613


How could it be that He who has created [all] should not know [all]? Yea, He alone is unfathomable [in His wisdom], aware! ^qAsad6613


Should He not know what He created? And He is the Subtile, the Aware. ^qPickthall6613


Shall He not know, who created? And He is the All-subtle, the All-aware. ^qArberry6613

# 15

Huwal lazee ja'ala lakumul arda zaloolan famshoo fee manaakibihaa wa kuloo mir rizqihee wa ilaihin nushoor ^qTrans6614


He it is who has made the earth easy to live upon: go about, then, in all its regions, and partake the sustenance which He provides: but [always bear in mind that] unto Him you shall be resurrected. ^qAsad6614


He it is Who hath made the earth subservient unto you, so Walk in the paths thereof and eat of His providence. And unto Him will be the resurrection (of the dead). ^qPickthall6614


It is He who made the earth submissive to you; therefore walk in its tracts, and eat of His provision; to Him is the Uprising. ^qArberry6614

# 16

'A-amintum man fissamaaa'i aiyakhsifa bi kumul arda fa izaa hiya tamoor ^qTrans6615


Can you ever feel secure that He who is in heaven will not cause the earth to swallow you up when, lo and behold, it begins to quake? ^qAsad6615


Have ye taken security from Him Who is in the heaven that He will not cause the earth to swallow you when lo! it is convulsed? ^qPickthall6615


Do you feel secure that He who is in heaven will not cause the earth to swallow you, the while it rocks? ^qArberry6615

# 17

Am amintum man fissamaaa'i ai yursila 'alaikum haasiban fasata'lamoona kaifa nazeer ^qTrans6616


Or can you ever feel secure that He who is in heaven will not let loose against you a deadly stormwind, whereupon you would come to know how [true] My warning was? ^qAsad6616


Or have ye taken security from Him Who is in the heaven that He will not let loose on you a hurricane? But ye shall know the manner of My warning. ^qPickthall6616


Do you feel secure that He who is in heaven will not loose against you a squall of pebbles, then you shall know how My warning is? ^qArberry6616

# 18

Wa laqad kazzabal lazeena min qablihim fakaifa kaana nakeer ^qTrans6617


And, indeed, [many of] those who lived aforetime did give the lie [to My warnings]: and how awesome was My rejection [of them]! ^qAsad6617


And verily those before them denied, then (see) the manner of My wrath (with them)! ^qPickthall6617


Those that were before them also cried lies; then how was My horror! ^qArberry6617

# 19

Awalam yaraw ilat tairi fawqahum saaaffaatinw wa yaqbidn; maa yumsikuhunna illaar rahmaan; innahoo bikulli shai im baseer ^qTrans6618


Have they, then, never beheld the birds above them, spreading their wings and drawing them in? None but the Most Gracious upholds them: for, verily, He keeps all things in His sight. ^qAsad6618


Have they not seen the birds above them spreading out their wings and closing them? Naught upholdeth them save the Beneficent. Lo! He is Seer of all things. ^qPickthall6618


Have they not regarded the birds above them spreading their wings, and closing them? Naught holds them but the All-merciful. Surely He sees everything. ^qArberry6618

# 20

Amman haazal lazee huwa jundul lakum yansurukum min doonir rahmaan; inilkaafiroona illaa fee ghuroor ^qTrans6619


[And] is there any, besides the Most Gracious, that could be a shield for you, and could succour you [against danger]? They who deny this truth are but lost in self- delusion! ^qAsad6619


Or who is he that will be an army unto you to help you instead of the Beneficent? The disbelievers are in naught but illusion. ^qPickthall6619


Or who is this that shall be a host for you to help you, apart from the All-merciful? The unbelievers are only in delusion. ^qArberry6619

# 21

Amman haazal lazee yarzuqukum in amsaka rizqah; bal lajjoo fee 'utuwwinw wa nufoor ^qTrans6620


Or is there any that could provide you with sustenance if He should withhold His provision [from you]? Nay, but they [who are bent on denying the truth] stubbornly persist in their disdain [of God's messages] and in their headlong flight [from Him]! ^qAsad6620


Or who is he that will provide for you if He should withhold His providence? Nay, but they are set in pride and frowardness. ^qPickthall6620


Or who is this that shall provide for you if He withholds His provision? No, but they persist in disdain and aversion. ^qArberry6620

# 22

Afamai yamshee mukibban 'alaa wajhihee ahdaaa ammany yamshee sawiyyan 'alaa siratim mustaqeem ^qTrans6621


But then, is he that goes along with his face close to the ground better guided than he that walks upright on a straight way? ^qAsad6621


Is he who goeth groping on his face more rightly guided, or he who walketh upright on a straight road? ^qPickthall6621


What, is he who walks prone upon his face better guided than he who walks upright on a straight path? ^qArberry6621

# 23

Qul huwal lazee ansha akum wa ja'ala lakumus sam'a wal absaara wal af'idata qaleelam maa tashkuroon ^qTrans6622


SAY: "[God is] He who has brought you [all] into being, and has endowed you with hearing, and sight, and hearts: [yet] how seldom are you grateful!" ^qAsad6622


Say (unto them, O Muhammad): He it is who gave you being, and hath assigned unto you ears and eyes and hearts. Small thanks give ye! ^qPickthall6622


Say: 'It is He who produced you, and appointed for you hearing and sight and hearts; little thanks you show!' ^qArberry6622

# 24

Qul huwal lazee zara akum fil ardi wa ilaihi tuhsharoon ^qTrans6623


Say: "It is He who has multiplied you on earth; and it is unto Him that you shall be gathered [on resurrection]." ^qAsad6623


Say: He it is Who multiplieth you in the earth, and unto Whom ye will be gathered. ^qPickthall6623


Say: 'It is He who scattered you in the earth, and unto Him you shall be mustered.' ^qArberry6623

# 25

Wa yaqooloona mataa haazal wa'du in kuntum saadiqeen ^qTrans6624


But they [only] ask, "When is this promise to be fulfilled? [Answer this, O you who believe in it,] if you are men of truth!" ^qAsad6624


And they say: When (will) this promise (be fulfilled), if ye are truthful? ^qPickthall6624


They say, 'When shall this promise come to pass, if you speak truly?' ^qArberry6624

# 26

Qul innamal 'ilmu 'indallaahi wa innamaaa ana nazeerum mubeen ^qTrans6625


Say thou, [O Prophet:] "Knowledge thereof rests with God alone; and I am only a plain warner." ^qAsad6625


Say: The knowledge is with Allah only, and I am but a plain warner; ^qPickthall6625


Say: 'The knowledge is with God; I am only a clear warner.' ^qArberry6625

# 27

Falaammaa ra-awhu zulfatan seee'at wujoohul lazeena kafaroo wa qeela haazal lazee kuntum bihee tadda'oon ^qTrans6626


Yet in the end, when they shall see that [fulfilment] close at hand, the faces of those who were bent on denying the truth will be stricken with grief; and they will be told, "This it is that you were [so derisively] calling for!" ^qAsad6626


But when they see it nigh, the faces of those who disbelieve will be awry, and it will be said (unto them): This is that for which ye used to call. ^qPickthall6626


Then, when they see it nigh at hand, the faces of the unbelievers will be vexed, and it will be said, 'This is what you were promised.' ^qArberry6626

# 28

Qul ara'aytum in ahlaka niyal laahu wa mam ma'iya aw rahimanaa famai-yujeerul kaafireena min 'azaabin aleem ^qTrans6627


SAY [O Prophet]: "What do you think? Whether God destroys me and those who follow me, or graces us with His mercy - is there anyone that could protect [you] deniers of the truth from grievous suffering [in the life to come]?" ^qAsad6627


Say (O Muhammad): Have ye thought: Whether Allah causeth me (Muhammad) and those with me to perish or hath mercy on us, still, who will protect the disbelievers from a painful doom? ^qPickthall6627


Say: 'What think you? If God destroys me and those with me, or has mercy on us, then who will protect the unbelievers from a painful chastisement?' ^qArberry6627

# 29

Qul huwar rahmaanu aamannaa bihee wa 'alaihi tawakkalnaa fasata'lamoona man huwa fee dalaalim mubeen ^qTrans6628


Say: "He is the Most Gracious: we have attained to faith in Him, and in Him have we placed our trust; and in time you will come to know which of us was lost in manifest error." ^qAsad6628


Say: He is the Beneficent. In Him we believe and in Him we put our trust. And ye will soon know who it is that is in error manifest. ^qPickthall6628


Say: 'He is the All-merciful. We believe in Him, and in Him we put all our trust. Assuredly, you will soon, know who is in manifest error. ^qArberry6628

# 30

Qul ara'aytum in asbaha maaa'ukum ghawran famai yaateekum bimaaa'im ma'een ^qTrans6629


Say [unto those who deny the truth]: "What do you think? If of a sudden all your water were to vanish underground, who [but God] could provide you with water from [new] unsullied springs?" ^qAsad6629


Say: Have ye thought: If (all) your water were to disappear into the earth, who then could bring you gushing water? ^qPickthall6629


Say: 'What think you? If in the morning your water should have vanished into the earth, then who would bring you running water?' ^qArberry6629
