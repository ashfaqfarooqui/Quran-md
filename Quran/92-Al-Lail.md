# 3

# 4

# 5

As for him who gives and is godfearing ^qArberry914

# 6

Wa saddaqa bil husnaa ^qTrans915


and believes in the truth of the ultimate good ^qAsad915


And believeth in goodness; ^qPickthall915


and confirms the reward most fair, ^qArberry915

# 7

for him shall We make easy the path towards [ultimate] ease. ^qAsad916


Surely We will ease his way unto the state of ease. ^qPickthall916


We shall surely ease him to the Easing. ^qArberry916

# 8

But as for him who is a miser, and self-sufficient, ^qArberry917

# 9

and calls the ultimate good a lie – ^qAsad918

# 10

We shall surely ease him to the Hardship; ^qArberry919

# 11

his wealth shall not avail him when he perishes. ^qArberry9110

# 12
